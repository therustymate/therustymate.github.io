## Note
I am releasing this confidential document because the following declassification conditions have been met:

* The vendor was contacted and patched the vulnerability. (Met)
* The information remained confidential for more than 90 days. (Met)

A bug bounty reward of NZD $320 was provided. On 2026-05-19, I confirmed that the business is currently operating normally on the platform and that my access to the security system has been removed. Because the business is now operating normally, I cannot test whether the vulnerability still exists. A mitigation had already been applied, but it was removed approximately twice by a senior administrator or a third party; after reporting this to senior management, it was applied again.

Since the conditions have been met and the business is operating normally, I am publicly disclosing the bug bounty report below. If you are associated to the vendor and the vulnerability has not been patched, please contact me immediately and patch the vulnerability as soon as possible. Even if this report is not the cause, this vulnerability can be quickly exploited. I strongly recommend patching it if the vulnerability is not patched.
